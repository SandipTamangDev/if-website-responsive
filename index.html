Nyisal Hostel – Empowering Himalayan Children Through Education

Our Mission

At Nyisal Hostel, we provide children from remote Nyisal village in Upper Dolpa with a loving home, food, shelter, education, and cultural learning in Kathmandu—helping them grow in knowledge, identity, and hope.

We are also working to create a sustainable source of income through small businesses to support the hostel’s long-term future.

Why We Exist

Nyisal is a small Himalayan village in Upper Dolpa, where access to education, health care, and basic facilities is extremely limited. To break this cycle of hardship, we brought 13 children to Kathmandu - offering them opportunity without letting them lose their culture. Every child in our care carries the dreams of a forgotten valley - and we are here to guide them toward a brighter, self-reliant future.

What We Do

For each child, we ensure:

  • Safe, nurturing accommodation
  • Admission to Shree Janakalyan School in Kathmandu
  • Daily meals, clothes, and school supplies
  • Medical care and emotional support
  • Tibetan language and cultural education
